Morning Lab

key stage two
10.00 - 13.00

Children will be introduced to Scratch and will be led through a series of fun challenges which aim to teach fundamental programming concepts.

These sessions are ideal for children at key stage 2 level, or for anyone who has never programmed before.

Over a number of weeks projects will become progressively more challenging and children may eventually be encouraged to progress onto Python.

Afternoon Lab

key stage three
14.00 - 17.00

Children will be introduced to Python, one of the most widely used programming languages with numerous applications and a large online community.

This session is ideal for children who have previous coding experience or are looking for an extension to scratch.

Over a number of weeks projects will become progressively more challenging and some topics may eventually reach GCSE level.
